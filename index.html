About Citronix

Established in 2000, Citronix is a world-class manufacturer of Continuous Inkjet and Thermal Inkjet printers headquartered in Texas, USA. Citronix's printers are sold through a strong network of distributors and are renowned in the industry for their simplicity, reliability, capability and excellent value that offer to our customers.

In India, Citronix began its operations in 2015 with a single distributor and today has more than eight distributors across the country.
